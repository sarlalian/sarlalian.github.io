Hugo

History of a blog Over the years I’ve maintained various blog’s using various engines, including Wordpress, Jekyll, Octopress and Blogger, and most recently my move to Hugo. Hugo, like Jekyll is a simple blog aware static website engine. With the larger, more comprehensive platforms, I always felt trapped and constrained within whatever themes were available, but with the static site generators it was easier to iterate on the design to get the look and effect that I wanted.
